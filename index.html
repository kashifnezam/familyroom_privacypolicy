Privacy Policy – Family Room App

Effective Date: 17 Feb 2025
Last Updated: 29 Dec 2025

Welcome to Family Room App. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile details (if provided)
  • User ID
  • Room / group related information

Location Data:

  • Foreground location
  • Background location (when enabled)

Location data is used to:

  • Show real-time location of room members
  • Enable live map view while chatting
  • Display movement history for up to 3 days
Location data is shared only with members of the same room.

Device & Usage Information:

  • Device model
  • Operating system
  • App version
  • IP address
  • MAC address
  • App usage logs
  • Crash reports
  • Last login timestamp

This data is collected for security, fraud prevention, and performance improvement.

Chat Information:

  • Text messages shared within rooms
  • Visible only to room members

Feature Requests & Feedback:

  • Feature description
  • Optional suggested contribution amount
  • User ID
  • Submission time

Media & Storage Access:

  • Profile pictures or uploaded images
  • Access requested only with user permission

2. Payment / UPI Support

IMPORTANT:

  • We do NOT collect payment data
  • We do NOT store UPI IDs or bank details
  • We do NOT store transaction IDs or references
  • We do NOT receive payment confirmations

Family Room uses a direct UPI deep link. All payment actions are handled entirely by the user’s UPI app. This feature exists only to support voluntary feature development.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve app functionality
  • Enable family location sharing
  • Enable communication features
  • Improve security and prevent misuse
  • Analyze performance and fix issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing of Information

We do NOT sell your personal data.

  • With room members (based on your actions)
  • With trusted service providers
  • When required by law or for user safety

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices & Rights

  • Disable location sharing from device settings
  • Access, update, or delete your information
  • Request account deletion via support

7. Children’s Privacy (Below 5 Years)

Family Room is designed for families, including children under 5 years old.

  • Accounts must be managed by a parent or guardian
  • No data collected without parental consent
  • Any such data will be deleted immediately

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the “Last Updated” date.
